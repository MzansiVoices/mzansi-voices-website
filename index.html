Mzansi Voices

Mzansi Voices is a coalition of formidable professionals comprising esteemed PhD holders, accomplished attorneys, ardent advocates, seasoned human resource experts, skilled public relations specialists, and innovative information technology strategists. We are dedicated to our land, country, and people, believing that an informed citizenry is an empowered citizenry.

Our initiatives include recruiting competent contributors, engaging in thoughtful discourse on legislation and policies related to migration, and effectively sharing knowledge online and with various organizations.

Our Initiatives

  • Recruiting Competent Contributors: We identify skilled researchers to benefit ordinary South Africans.
  • Engaging in Thoughtful Discourse: We create platforms for discussions on migration legislation and policies.
  • Effective Knowledge-Sharing: We make our research findings accessible to the broader public.

We review legislation such as the Constitution of South Africa, Immigration Act of 2002, Refugee Act of 1998, and international conventions like the 1951 UN Convention on Refugees. We also compile rebuttal reports to ensure transparent and unbiased discussions on migration.
